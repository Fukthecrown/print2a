Print Information:

-Hatchbox PLA (black)
-0.2mm layer height
-0.4mm nozzle (most printers use this standard)
-100% perimeters/walls, you shouldn't need any normal infill
-Whatever temperature PLA prints best on your printer
-No supports
-Cone-up orientation
